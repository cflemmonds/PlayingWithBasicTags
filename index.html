I bet you're wondering, "What outfit did you wear?". The Bordeaux colorway is a tough one to pull off. Matching the color has a high barrier to entry; you are bound to struggle if your color matching skills aren't up to par. Is it maroon? Is it burgundy? Some will claim they see a deep red, others will say it's a weird shade of purple. Lucky for you, I've got a winning formula that will have you looking stylish while avoiding the pitfall of matching the exact shade.

    How to build an outfit

  1. Start with the shoes. If the color is a tough match, find one that complements it. More on that later.
  2. Use that color and pick a top. Hoodie? Sure. Graphic tee? Knock yourself out. Polo? Oh you fancy, huh?
  3. Finally, find a nice pair of pants. Try not to overpower the color of the shoes and top with the pants.
